Article Title

In HTML, the <article> element is a semantic tag used to define a self-contained piece of content that could be distributed and reused independently. It's typically used for content such as blog posts, forum posts, news articles, or any other independent content item within a webpage.

Section Title

In HTML, the <section> element is a semantic tag used to define sections within a document or webpage. It is typically employed to group together related content. The purpose of <section> is to improve the structure and semantics of the HTML document, making it more accessible to users and aiding search engines in understanding the content.
